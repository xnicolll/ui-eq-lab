EQ (Equalization) is the process of adjusting the balance of frequency components within an audio signal.

Think of it like shaping the sound.

With EQ, you can:

  • Turn up the bass
  • Soften harsh treble
  • Carve out space in a mix

When mixing music, EQ helps instruments and vocals sit well together without clashing.

Mixing: the process of balancing, blending, and / or applying effects to individual audio tracks to create a cohesive sound.

The frequency range is split into three main groups:

Lows
20 Hz - 250 Hz
Bass, Kick Drum
Mids
250 Hz - 4 kHz
Vocals, Guitar
Highs
4 kHz - 20 kHz
Cymbals, Hi-hat
